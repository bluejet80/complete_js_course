Infinity Begins

Infinity begins in an environment overcome by light. Nothing is distinguishable, all is ambiguous.

A great way of clearing out the mind is to imagine yourself as a rock. Returning to th origin allows us to approach each idea unhindered by outside considerations.

The mind is inside itself now and acts with absolute freedom. It defines the world. It names the animals and defines the forces of nature in its own unique way.
